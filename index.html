Mbinu

The Eco Ops App is designed to support community level validation of carbon credit earning actions.
  1. Add witnesses to document work your/ your group's work.
  2. Log your carbon negative actions ( compost, plastics diversion, plant nursery, trees, crop improvement, etc.
  3. Review your reports, customize them.
  4. Customize your project support & carbon credit sales page.
  5. Add your mpesa/ paypal/ or crypto payout settings.
